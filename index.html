WatchMyCalendar

Privacy Policy

Calendar Data

WatchMyCalendar accesses calendar information solely to display upcoming events on your Wear OS smartwatch.

Calendar data remains on your devices and is not transmitted to servers operated by the developer.

Data Collection

WatchMyCalendar does not collect personal data. However, anonymous crash reports are collected to improve app stability (see below).

No Tracking

  • No analytics
  • No advertising
  • No user accounts

Crash Reporting

WatchMyCalendar uses Firebase Crashlytics (Google) to collect anonymous crash reports. This helps us identify and fix stability issues.

Crash reports may include:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • Stack trace of the crash

No personal data, calendar content, or event details are included in crash reports. Data is processed by Google in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy.
